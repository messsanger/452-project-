According to the latest data, in December 2023, Asus Group's revenue reached NT $36.043 billion, a monthly decrease of 16.47% and an annual decrease of 12.12%. In the fourth quarter, the group's revenue was 121.317 billion yuan, a decrease of 10.17% compared to the previous quarter and a year-on-year decrease of 4.53%. The annual cumulative revenue was 483.471 billion yuan, a decrease of 10%. In terms of brand, the revenue in December was 32 billion yuan, a decrease of 15% year-on-year, and the total revenue for the year was 448 billion yuan, a decrease of 9% year-on-year.

Recently, the Australian computer sales market has shown new trends and changes. Internationally renowned brands such as Apple, Dell, HP, etc. still hold important positions in the Australian market. These brands have won widespread recognition and trust from consumers due to their strong brand influence, advanced technology, and high-quality product quality. In addition, some emerging brands have also emerged in the market, gradually establishing a good brand image in the minds of consumers through innovative product design and marketing strategies.
